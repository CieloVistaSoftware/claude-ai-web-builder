    Planned Services

    Life-Saving Services for Kenya

    We're building infrastructure that will connect people to help when they need it most, through these three core services.

    One-Tap Emergency

    Fast, reliable help when seconds matter

    When launched, our platform will instantly connect you to ambulance, police, or fire services with GPS-enabled technology that brings the closest help to you, fast.

    • GPS-location tracking
    • Verified responders network
    • Real-time status updates
    • Will be completely free for all users

    Planned launch: Q4 2025

    24/7 Mental Health

    Support when you need someone to talk to

    Our upcoming mental health service will provide access to a supportive AI therapy chatbot, completely private and anonymous. Get help with anxiety, grief, stress, or trauma.

    • Will be available 24/7
    • Complete privacy & anonymity
    • AI-powered support
    • Will be free for everyone

    Currently in development

    Roadside Assistance

    Help when you're stranded on the road

    Our future roadside service will provide fuel delivery, towing, or minor repairs when stranded. Kenya's growing number of car owners will be able to access help on demand.

    • Quick response times
    • Verified mechanics network
    • Affordable pricing
    • Freemium model

    Planned for after emergency services launch

    Police Emergency Response

    Swift security assistance when you need it most

    Our platform will connect you with nearby police services, providing quick response to security concerns and emergencies.

    Fire Emergency Services

    Rapid response to fire incidents

    Our network will include fire response teams, ensuring quick action to protect lives and property during fire emergencies.

    Our Story

    The Founder's Journey

    Pinpoint was born from a deeply personal experience. After witnessing slow, unresponsive emergency services in real-life situations, Bryan Migot was determined to build a system where help is just one tap awayregardless of who you are or where you live.

    "When help didn't come, I decided to build it. Every life matters, and no one should suffer because help was delayed or unavailable."
    BM
    Bryan Migot, Founder & CEO

    There are no venture backers, no large teams, and no government partnerships yet. Just one founder, driven by urgency and belief, building what he couldn't find when people needed it most.

    Bryan Migot

    Founder & CEO
